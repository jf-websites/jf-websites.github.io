The Mobile-First Approach

A "mobile-first" approach involves designing a desktop site starting with the mobile version, which is then adapted to larger screens (contrary to the traditional approach of starting with a desktop site and then adapting it to smaller screens). Generally speaking, a mobile-first approach means building your website with your mobile users in mind.

Mobile accounts for approximately half of web traffic worldwide. In the second quarter of 2019, mobile devices (exluding tablets) generated 48.91 percent of global website traffic, consistently hovering around the the 50 percent mark since the beginning of 2017. Due to low infrastructure and financial restraints, many emerging digital markets skipped the desktop internet phase entirely and moved straight onto mobile internet via smartphone and tablet devices.
