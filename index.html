The Mobile-First Approach

     Mobile accounts for approximately 3/4 of web traffic worldwide. In the fourth quarter of 2019, mobile devices (exluding tablets) generated 76.21 percent of global website traffic. Due to low infrastructure and financial restraints, many emerging digital markets skipped the desktop internet phase entirely and moved straight onto mobile internet via smartphone and tablet devices.

     A "mobile-first" approach involves designing a desktop site starting with the mobile version, which is then adapted to larger screens (contrary to the traditional approach of starting with a desktop site and then adapting it to smaller screens). Generally speaking, a mobile-first approach means building your website with your mobile users in mind.
